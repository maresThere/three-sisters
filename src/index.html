Camp

Camps for girls are offered during summer and winter break. There is maximum of 6 riders per session. Each child is given their own horse for a week. All aspects of horse ownership and care are taught. The hours are 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. Monday - Friday with before and after camp care available. Lunch, snacks and lots of fluids are provided.

Lessons

Lessons are offered Monday - Friday in the afternoon and all day on Sundays. Students will learn horse grooming, riding and tack care. Our desire is to instill a passion for the animal into others. We specialize in the younger equestrians!

Boarding

  • Owner on-site 24 hour supervision

  • Farrier on-site every 3 weeks & on call

  • Premium 12% pellet feed, T&A hay, beet pulp & alfalfa cubes.

  • Vetrinarian on-site twice a year for scheduled shots, coggins and vaccines.
